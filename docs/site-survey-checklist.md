# Site Survey Checklist - Mounts Bay Road

Use this checklist when scouting locations for sensor deployment.

## Route Overview

**Mounts Bay Road** runs along the Swan River from UWA to the Narrows Bridge (~3km).

```
West                                                            East
[UWA] ─── [Matilda Bay] ─── [Crawley Bay] ─── [Kings Park] ─── [Narrows]
 PTW-001      PTW-002          PTW-003          PTW-003         PTW-004
```

Target: 4 sensors, roughly evenly spaced along the route.

## Recommended Locations to Survey

### 1. Near UWA (Western end)
- **Area:** Hackett Drive / Mounts Bay Road intersection
- **Look for:** UWA boat sheds, private properties on Hackett Dr
- **Coordinates:** Approx -31.9815, 115.8175

### 2. Matilda Bay
- **Area:** Near Matilda Bay Restaurant / boat ramp
- **Look for:** Restaurant property, nearby residences
- **Coordinates:** Approx -31.9785, 115.8245

### 3. Crawley Bay / Kings Park
- **Area:** Near the Old Swan Brewery site
- **Look for:** Commercial properties, park infrastructure
- **Coordinates:** Approx -31.9665, 115.8395

### 4. Near Narrows (Eastern end)
- **Area:** Just before Narrows interchange
- **Look for:** Commercial buildings on south side
- **Coordinates:** Approx -31.9595, 115.8525
